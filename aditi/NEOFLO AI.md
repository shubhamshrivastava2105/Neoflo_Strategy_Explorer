**PROCURE TO PAY**  
**(P2P)**

### **P2P Workflow — Detailed Breakdown**

① Invoice Processing Automation

* Invoices received via mails / portals / EDI / upload  
* AI OCR extracts all data  
* Validates against PO, vendor master  
* Automatically sends invoice to correct person  
* Approves 3-way match or payment

*Companies: Peakflo, HighRadius, Stampli, Basware, Aspire, Neoflo, Medius*

---

② 3-Way Matching & Exception Handling

* Pull PO, GRN, invoice for same transaction  
* Compare all  
* Auto approve if error is within threshold  
* Agentic AI → checks ERP history, contacts vendor in case of mismatch  
* Complex error goes to humans

*Companies: Peakflo, HighRadius, Coupa, SAP Ariba, Neoflo*

---

③ Payment Execution & Scheduling

* Approved invoices grouped into payment batch  
* Payment rail / channel selected  
* Schedule deadline  
* Review & release  
* Confirm to vendor \+ post to ERP

*Companies: Peakflo, Tipalti, Aspire, Airwallex, Ramp, Nium, Coupa Pay*

### **ORDER TO CASH** 

**(O2C)**

### **O2C Workflows — Detailed Breakdown**

#### ① Cash Application

* Payment lands to bank account  
* AI reads emails, EDI, lockbox file  
* Matches payment amount \= invoice  
* Handles payment (over, under, partial)  
* Post matched payment to ERP, closes invoice

*Companies: HighRadius, Billtrust, Esker, Peakflo, YayPay*

---

#### ② Billing & Invoice

* Order → automatic create → invoice  
* Generates correct invoice  
* Delivers via customer preferred channel  
* Payment links embedded  
* System tracks payment & sends reminders

*Companies: Aspire, Peakflo, Chargebee, Stripe, Esker*

---

#### ③ Collection Management

* System AI scores each overdue by risk & priority  
* Keep sending reminders  
* AI generates calls / msg / SMS / emails → ERP logs  
* Customer promise to pay logged & tracked  
* Dispute raised → dunning paused → manual arms

*Companies: Peakflo, HighRadius, Gaviti, YayPay*

### ④ Credit Risk Management

* New customer applies for credit  
* Collect previous history of customer  
* AI calculates → credit score  
* System continuously monitors customer  
* Auto hold on order if limit exceeds

### 

  *Companies :- HighRadius, Sidetrade,Creditsafe,Esker*

---

### ⑤ Deduction & Dispute Management

* Customer pays less than invoiced  
* AI identifies deduction type from remittance & history  
* Routes issue to correct team  
* Team investigates & resolves  
* Deduction / issue closed  
  *Companies:-Billtrust,Peakflo,Esker,ModelN,HighRadius*

**RECORD TO REPORT**  
**(R2R)**

### **R2R Workflows — Detailed Breakdown**

#### ① Bank Account Reconciliation

* ERP automatically receives daily bank transactions from bank  
* AI compares bank transaction \= company accounting records  
* Unmatch → leads to review  
* Finance team fixes it  
* System creates report after everything

*Companies: HighRadius, Aspire, Blackline, Neoflo, Xero, Netsuite*

---

#### ② Financial Close Automation

* AI automatically collects all financial data  
* System automatically creates accounting journal entries  
* Prepayments  
* Intercompany balances matched & eliminated  
* Trial balance generated & reviewed by controller  
* Compares actual result, budget for CFO commentary

*Companies: HighRadius, Blackline, Peakflo, Neoflo, FloQast, Trintech*

---

####  ③ Spend Analytics & Reporting

* System collects all expenditure at 1 place  
* AI automatically organises expense in categories  
* Dashboard comparison  
* AI detects suspicious expenses  
* System automatically creates report for CFO  
  *Companies: HighRadius, Blackline, Peakflo, Neoflo, FloQast, Trintech*
