About

What Neoflo is.

Neoflo is a multi-workflow FinOps platform for finance teams in the mid-market. We unify three jobs that controllers run today across 3–5 disconnected systems — Procure-to-Pay (AP), Order-to-Cash (AR / collections), and Record-to-Report (close & reconciliation) — onto one workflow engine with one vendor master, one customer master, and one audit trail.

What it is

An AI-native, multi-workflow FinOps platform — AP, AR, and close on a single engine. Extraction trained on Southeast Asian invoice formats, GL coding suggestions, anomaly detection, and voice AI for collections. ASEAN e-invoicing native (MyInvois, InvoiceNow, e-Faktur). Engineering delivered from India for cost leverage in our target markets.

What it does

Replaces the patchwork mid-market controllers run today — ERP + an AP point tool + an AR / collections tool + spreadsheet reconciliations + email / Slack approvals. Connects to the ERP rather than replacing it. The wedge is P2P invoice processing; from there we expand into O2C (cash application, deductions, dunning) and R2R (close, recs, intercompany). 28 sub-workflows mapped across the three processes.

Who it's for

Mid-market companies — $50M–$500M revenue, 10–50 finance staff, multi-entity, ERP-connected, currently stitched together on Excel + email + bandaid SaaS. Two go-to-market priorities: SEA mid-market (under-served — Peakflo is our only Core competitor) and SAP-shop enterprise (where VIM and Ariba are too heavy or stale). US served only via warm CPA-partner channels.

Where we are today
Live: Zalora (P2P, our SEA reference).  ·  Validation deal in flight: Spectrum Brands ($2.8B NYSE-listed) — rejected a $1.2–1.5M HighRadius quote for deductions and cash application; they are our proof that "AP + AR + close at ~1/10 the cost" is the bet.  ·  Pipeline: 25 prospect briefs documented across SEA, SAP-shop enterprise, and warm-channel US.
